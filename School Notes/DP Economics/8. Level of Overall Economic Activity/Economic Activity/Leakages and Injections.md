##### Leakages and Injections 
- leakages that go out of the flow come back in as injections
- tho might not be equal
- If ==Leakages > Injections==: Income flow ↓
- If ==Leakages < Injections==: Income flow ↑

Leakages | Injections
--- | ---
saving | investment
taxes | government spending
import spending | export spending
