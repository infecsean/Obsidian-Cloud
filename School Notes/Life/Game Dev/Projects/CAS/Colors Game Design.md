### Colors
- We associate colors with the things we see them the most with
- Green/Red: Health, green for when full, red when die
- Yellow/White: Navigation, either through maps or blended in levels
- Blue: Magic
- Purple: CORRUPTION, danger (but not purely, not exclusively)
